Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create a workout plan?
Go to the "Plans" tab, tap Add Plan, then add your workouts, exercises, and sets.

2. Are the exercise videos free?
Yes, all videos displayed in Trackletics are streamed directly from YouTube.

3. Do I need an internet connection?
An internet connection is required to view exercise videos.

Delete Your Trackletics Account

You can permanently delete your Trackletics account at any time directly from within the app.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  • Open the Trackletics app.
  • Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  • Confirm your choice.

Once confirmed, your account and all related personal information will be permanently deleted.

Data Deleted

  • Personal information (e.g., name, email, gender)
  • Account details and preferences
  • Activity history inside the app

Data Retained

No personal data is retained after deletion. Trackletics does not store purchase history or analytics data.

Important: Once your account is deleted, it cannot be restored. You may create a new account at any time.
